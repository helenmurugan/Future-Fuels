Why Future Fuels?

If you are interested in learning about the future of the transportation industry, you have come to the right place! Future Fuels keeps up-to-speed with the latest scientific research in the transportation industry and provides an informative and unbiased overview of alternative fuels, for road, air and water transport.

What's Wrong With Petrol and Diesel?

Combustion of fossil fuels such as petrol and diesel produce CO2 and other emissions into the atmosphere which contribute towards global warming. We will need alternative fuels or methods to power our cars, which do not produce harmful emissions.

In response to European targets to reduce CO2 emissions, a law has been introduced banning the sale of new petrol and diesel cars from 2035.
