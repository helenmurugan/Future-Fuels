Future Fuels helps businesses adopt zero-emissions solutions for road transport

Why Future Fuels?

In response to European targets to reduce CO2 emissions, a law has been introduced banning the sale of new petrol and diesel cars from 2035 in the EU. Similar bans on the sale of fossil-fuel powered vans and HGVs will follow shortly, as governments bring in new legislation to clean up their emissions.

Future Fuels helps businesses and other organisations adapt to this new legislation, finding the best alternative fuels to power zero-emissions fleets whether it be batteries, biofuels or hydrogen.
